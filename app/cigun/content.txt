Title: 300 Questions about Qigong
Author: Qigong Master

Chapter 1: Understanding Qi
Qi is the vital energy that flows through all living things. It is the foundation of Qigong practice.

Chapter 2: The Three Dantians
The three dantians are the energy centers of the body: Lower Dantian (Physical), Middle Dantian (Emotional), and Upper Dantian (Spiritual).

Chapter 3: Breathing Techniques
Deep abdominal breathing is essential for cultivating Qi. Inhale deeply through the nose, expanding the lower abdomen, and exhale slowly.

Chapter 4: Basic Stances
- Wuji Stance: Standing meditation for grounding.
- Horse Stance: Strengthening the legs and accumulating energy.

Chapter 5: Moving Qigong
- Cloud Hands: Promotes smooth flow of Qi.
- Gathering the Sky: Connecting with heavenly energy.
